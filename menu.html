How to Play

Select a stage and get ready for action!

-- Aim Blaster (Mouse Move)

-- Fire Blaster (Left Click)

When your blaster is empty click on the flashing blaster icon to reload

Your score is based on 10 points per target hit and then multiplied by your remaining time

On mobile devices please rotate your device to landscape as this app is optimised for 16/9
